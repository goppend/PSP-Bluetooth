Hi, I'm Stephen and the creator of the PSP Bluetooth project. This was something I started for fun going on three years ago.

⚠ Disclaimer ⚠

I know little about hardware, you are free to do as you wish with this project. Sell it, modify it, ignore it and move on. But do so with the knowledge it could work perfectly or could set your house on fire.

Initially it targeted the Arduino Nano 33 IOT and the primary goal was to play my PS1 and PSP games on the TV with a Bluetooth controller It has since grown into something far larger. Now based on the ESP32 with the ability to use as a consolizer but also as a portable (like the Nintendo Switch)

In its final form we also have this web app which provides the ability to program the ESP32 either over USB via serial or using Bluetooth for OTA. Not just that but it also provides configurable button mappings.
